Welcome to TeenagerPlaybook

Before you enter, please review our policies

🍪 Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser local storage to keep your session active for up to 7 days if you choose "Remember Me." No third-party tracking cookies are used.

📷 Camera & Microphone Access

Certain exercises request access to your camera (body postures, facial expressions) or microphone (intonation). Access is only requested during active exercises and is never recorded or stored on our servers.

🔒 Data & Privacy

Your account data (email, username, hashed password) is stored in a secure MongoDB database accessible only by the administrator. Your exercise history and accuracy scores are stored per-session and linked to your account for progress tracking.

📋 Terms of Use

This platform is for educational purposes only. You must be a registered user to access exercises. The administrator reserves the right to remove accounts that misuse the platform.

🎓 AI Evaluation

AI tools are used to evaluate your performance during exercises. Results are indicative and for learning purposes only. A minimum 70% accuracy threshold is used as a guide, not a definitive assessment.

You must accept all three to proceed.
