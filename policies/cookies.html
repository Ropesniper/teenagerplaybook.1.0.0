Cookie & Storage Policy

How we use browser storage on your device

Overview

TeenagerPlaybook does not use traditional HTTP cookies. Instead, we use browser-native storage mechanisms (localStorage and sessionStorage) to maintain your session and cache your exercise data locally.

1. Session Storage (sessionStorage)

Used when you log in without selecting "Remember me":

  • Stores your authentication token for the current browser session
  • Automatically cleared when the browser tab or window is closed
  • Cleared on logout

2. Local Storage (localStorage) — Remember Me

Used when you select "Remember me for 7 days":

  • Stores your authentication token with a 7-day expiry timestamp
  • Stores your username and role for UI personalization
  • Automatically invalidated after 7 days
  • Cleared immediately on logout

3. Exercise History Cache

Your exercise history (session type, accuracy scores, timestamps) is cached in localStorage for:

  • Offline history access when the server is unavailable
  • Instant display of the History page without server delay
  • Merging with server-side records when connectivity is restored

This data can be cleared at any time from the History page.

4. Consent Flag

A consent flag is stored in sessionStorage to prevent the consent popup from appearing repeatedly within the same browsing session. This flag does not contain any personal information.

5. No Third-Party Tracking

We do not use any advertising cookies, analytics tracking cookies, or third-party session identifiers. No data from your browser storage is shared with external services.

6. How to Clear Storage

You can clear all TeenagerPlaybook storage at any time by:

  • Logging out (clears authentication tokens)
  • Using the "Clear All History" button in the History page
  • Clearing your browser's local/session storage in browser settings
