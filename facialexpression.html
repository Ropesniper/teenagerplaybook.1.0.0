Before You Begin — Facial Expression Session

What this session requires:

📷 Camera access — your face is analysed in real time. Video never leaves your browser.

🤖 AI face analysis — TinyFaceDetector, SSD MobileNet, and MediaPipe Face Mesh evaluate your expressions simultaneously.

💡 Good lighting — face a light source (window or lamp in front of you). Do not sit with a bright window behind you.

🪑 Seated, face visible — sit upright with your face clearly visible and centred in camera. Keep your full face in frame.

😶 Clear face — remove glasses if possible, keep hair away from face for best landmark tracking.

You need 80% or above to pass each exercise. Exercise 3 requires holding a Duchenne smile for 15 seconds continuously.
